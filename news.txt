Out of form. Out of luck. Out of favour. The past three years in Virat Kohli's life have been anything but rosy. The crown slipped away from the king, inch by inch, until the feisty batter finally got the monkey off his back by ending a prolonged century drought in a T20I against Afghanistan. Heading into the World Cup, it has been a timely return to form for Kohli. He has also been backed through some difficult times by the team management
